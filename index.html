About Company

An efficient
Furniture designing team

Zeepiwoh Creations is a leisure and office furniture manufacturing business which specialises in high end pieces. Our services extend to both trade members and the general public (residential, corporate, hospitality, as well as retail architecture) and we treat all projects – big or small – with the same professionalism and commitment. We strive to be the best at what we do by offering you value for money, exceptional service and premium-quality products that speak volumes with their originality and aesthetic appeal. We custom make innovative and comfortable furniture for the entire house from the bedroom to the kitchen to the patio. Our ranges of office furniture are extensive and carefully designed to suit your needs. We custom make furniture to suit your space and style Zeepiwoh Creations focuses on clever space planning that will achieve optimum utilisation of your floor space.

Our success continues because of the team of dedicated and professional employees, who espouse passion for excellent furniture design, operational excellence and most importantly, delighted customers.
